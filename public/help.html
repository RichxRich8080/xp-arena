Account Protocols

Navigate to the Signup portal, choose a unique operative designation (Username) and secure credentials. After verification, visit your Profile to manage your AXP, Vault, and History.

The Vault stores your peak performance sensitivity cards. Presets allow for instant recalibration during high-stakes tournaments and scrims, ensuring consistent lethality.

Competitive Systems

Operatives ascend the Global Rankings by earning AXP (Arena Experience Points) through participation. Top Arenis are recognized weekly. In technical ties, the operative who achieved the score first retains priority.

Clan Wars require an active Clan affiliation. Clan Leaders can apply for war status via the War Command Center. Once active, all members contribute to the war score through specified activity.

Troubleshooting

Ensure session cookies are authorized for localhost or xp-arena.com. Clear browser cache if assets fail to load. If credentials remain invalid, initiate a secure reset via the Forgot Password protocol.
