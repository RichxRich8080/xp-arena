Help & FAQ

Everything you need to know to get the most out of XP ARENA.

How do I create an account?

Go to Signup, choose a unique username and password. After logging in, visit Profile to see AXP, Vault, Presets and History.

Why save Vault and Presets?

Vault keeps your best sensitivity cards. Presets let you reload successful combinations fast for tournaments and scrims.

How does the Leaderboard work?

Arenis rise by AXP earned via activity. Top Arenis are highlighted weekly. In case of ties, earlier timestamps rank higher.

Having trouble logging in?

Make sure your browser allows cookies and you’re using the correct username. If the issue persists, contact us on the Contact page.
